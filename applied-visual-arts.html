Applied Visual Arts

Concept Overview

Applied Visual Arts is booklet that promotes all the different art and design programs offered at Spokane Falls Community College. For this project, I created a logo and emblem design for the booklet cover that would represent the diversity of creative disciplines within the Applied Visual Arts department. The design needed to be bold, colorful, and visually engaging to capture the attention of prospective students and convey the dynamic nature of the programs offered.
