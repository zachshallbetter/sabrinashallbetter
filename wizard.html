The Wizard Project

Concept Overview

For this school project, the class was tasked with creating a design that can be used for a specialty promotion item. The client had asked that the design only be in two colors. The specialty promotion items were meant to be shared with industry professionals who spoke to our program.

What was once a doodle became one of my most iconic pieces during my time at Spokane Falls Community College. His original phrase, “Walla Kazoo, Walla Kazam! Graphic Design is my Jam!”, was meant to be a whimsical design that could be slapped on any computer or water bottle. Now, he’s just a silly little guy!
