Puff Palace

Concept Overview

Puff Palace is a cream puff boutique where every pastry feels like a tiny royal treasure. We specialize in light, cloud-soft cream puffs made with high-quality ingredients inspired by fairytales, elegance, and cozy indulgence.

The design features a custom pink outer carton adorned with delicate lace patterns and a custom logo typeface. Inside, individual cream puffs are housed in beautifully illustrated boxes colored in pink, chocolate, lavendar, and green tea, each decorated with matching character graphics.
