Puff Palace

Concept Overview

Puff Palace is a cream puff boutique where tasty pastry specialties reign supreme. For this branding and packaging design project, the task was to design a beautiful, elegant, and playful box packaging system that would hold three delicious cream puff treats.

The design features a custom pink, castle-inspired outer carton adorned with delicate lace patterns and a custom logo typeface. Inside, individual cream puffs are housed in beautifully illustrated sliding drawers colored in warm peach and royal lavender, each decorated with matching character graphics.
