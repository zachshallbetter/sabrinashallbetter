Preflight 2025 Project

Concept Overview

AAF Spokane holds an annual student portfolio review event called Preflight. For this project, I created a poster design to promote the 2025 Preflight event. The design features pastel colored keycaps that resemble building blocks. It's meant to highlight the idea of building a strong foundation in design principles and skills, which is essential for students preparing to enter the design industry. The playful and colorful aesthetic is intended to capture the attention of students and encourage them to participate in the event.
